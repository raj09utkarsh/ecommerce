ABOUT US

Exponential Classes is a premier independent institute of IIT-JEE and NEET Coaching founded on the core principles of excellence, quality, productivity, and performance to guide aspirant students through the journey of these prestigious national-level examinations and to prepare them to achieve a 100% guaranteed success.

We are one of the institutes located in Patna that provides coaching to students in grade 11th, 12th and 13th. Our institute brings you the talented combination of two brilliant faculties - Mr. Saurabh Kumar and Mr. Mithlesh Kumar Malakar who have transformed the lives of hundreds of students and always aspiring to help many more such ambitious students.

Mr. Saurabh Kumar is an ex-faculty of the legacy IIT-JEE Coaching Institute - Bansal classes of Kota. He did his graduation from The LNM Institute of Information Technology, Jaipur. He is known amongst his students for his skills of explaining concepts in a way that are handily grasped by students and his tips and tricks have been very useful to students in their examinations.

Mr. Mithlesh Kumar has been a faculty at Allen Institute in the past for a brief time and has gained high fame among his students with the alias "MK Sir". He has done his Bachelors in Science from M.U.

Our mission is to strive the career path of our students towards a guaranteed result. We wish you a brighter future and hope that you choose us to see a miracle in your performance. We just want you to do persistant hardwork and rest will be sone by our faculty's intelligence and experience.
